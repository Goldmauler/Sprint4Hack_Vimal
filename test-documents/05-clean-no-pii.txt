STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Revision 3.1 — For internal distribution only

PURPOSE

This document defines procedures for document review workflows. It applies to all review stages and contains no personally identifying information.

SCOPE

This procedure applies to all departments involved in processing. No names, contact details, financial identifiers, or medical records are referenced here.

STEPS

Step 1: Receive the document batch from the upstream queue.
Step 2: Classify using the automated classification engine.
Step 3: Apply redaction rules appropriate to the document class.
Step 4: Route flagged items to the human review queue.
Step 5: Reviewer confirms or overrides each flagged item.
Step 6: Approve and export the final document.

REVIEW CYCLE

This procedure is reviewed annually. Changes require approval from the relevant oversight committee. No individual identifiers are referenced here.

Version history is maintained in a separate tracking system.
